About

Campaigns are living stories, but managing them often feels like bookkeeping. Taking notes, tracking NPCs, and remembering open plot lines can pull focus away from the story itself.

We’re building an AI-assisted platform that keeps the story straight, offers flavorful exposition when you need it, and helps players stay focused on what matters most: telling stories together.
